Let's take a closer look at what was said, and how Melania reacted to her troubling US move because of immigration issues.

Melania's Ex Jure Zorc Revealed Her Interest At The Time Was Living Abroad In Italy Or France, Pursuing Fashion

Who was Melania before Donald? Well, Melania Trump's ex-boyfriend detailed whether she wanted a life in the US. Jure Zorc revealed that this wasn't on Melania's priority list. Instead, she dreamed of pursuing a life of fashion over in Italy or France. Zorc revealed, “She wanted to live abroad, in Italy, France, a life of fashion. She was very fashionable."

Zorc went on to reveal that a life in the US was far from her mind at the time. “I think it was very far from our minds at that time.”

As for how the two met, Melania's ex claims it was like a scene out of a movie,

“We met each other like in a movie,” he added. “I was on my motorbike, and she has been walking down a street. As I passed by her, I had a moment and thought, ‘Wow, who is this girl? She is so beautiful, I must turn back and follow her.'”

Much to her ex's surprise, Melania ended up moving to the US. However, according to Melania herself, the immigration process wasn't the easiest of situations.

Melania Revealed That Moving To The US Was A Difficult Experience

Although Melania was open to several opportunities in the US, the process of immigration wasn't an easy one. Melania says it, "opened my eyes to the harsh realities people face."

Melania revealed, "My personal experience of traversing the challenges of the immigration process opened my eyes to the harsh realities people face, including you, who try to become U.S. citizens," the former first lady said. "And then, of course, there are nuances of understanding the United States immigration laws and the complex legal language."

Donald Trump Was On A Date With Another Model When He First Met Melania

Both Donald and Melania recall the first time they met. Melania herself called their first encounter inappropriate, and we completely understand why. Donald was on a date with another model when he first met Melania.

Donald recalls, "I went crazy. I was actually supposed to meet somebody else," Trump recalled with Larry King. "There was this great supermodel sitting next to Melania. I was supposed to meet this supermodel. They said, 'Look, there's so and so.' I said, 'Forget about her. Who is the one on the left? And it was Melania."

Melania did not give Trump her number. Instead, Melania asked for his, to see what kind of phone number he'd give her.

"He wanted my number, but he was with a date, so of course I didn't give it to him. I said, 'I am not giving you my number; you give me yours, and I will call you.' I wanted to see what kind of number he would give me—if it was a business number, what is this? I'm not doing business with you."

Trump gave Melania four different phone numbers, which really showed his interest early on. It ended up being Melania's first date since arriving in New York.
