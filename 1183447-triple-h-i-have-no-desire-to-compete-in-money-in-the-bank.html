Triple H: I Have No Desire To Compete In A Money In The Bank Ladder Match

On tonight’s SmackDown, Triple H will be featured as part of his 25th-anniversary celebration. However, we are also on the road to WWE Money in the Bank. Over the years, Triple H has been involved in many gimmick matches but he has never been involved in a Money in the Bank Ladder Match.

Speaking with Newsweek, Triple H said he has no desire to ever compete in a Money in the Bank Match although he does say he’s never regretted any of the ladder matches he’s been involved with over the years.

“I have no desire to climb a high ladder and get pushed off of it. There are certain guys that are built for ladders and guys who are and I’m not one of them,” Triple H said. “My ladder matches have normally involved me whacking someone with it or getting whacked with it a lot. So climbing and falling off of it is not my forte.

He continued, “When the MITB ladder matches started to come around, it was built around an additional match at WrestleMania. I’ve really just had these other storylines going on where I was never sort of involved in the MITB matches themselves. I’ve had ladder matches and been involved with a lot of them. It’s never anything that I regret because you’ve never seen me do a moonsault for a reason.”
