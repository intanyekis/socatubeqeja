Jill Duggar Kids: Meet Jill Duggars Children Samuel Scott Dillard And Israel David Dillard

At the time of their June 2014 wedding, Derrick Dillard was 25 years old and Jill Duggar was 23 years old.

On February 18, 2014, Derick proposed to Jill and asked for her hand in marriage. He hired singer-songwriter Walker Hayes to write the song “When He Made You” on March 29, 2014, which he sang during their proposal.

He also brought Jill to a Mexican restaurant for lunch before proposing. This proposal was featured in the episode “The Proposal” of 19 Kids and Counting.

Meet Jill Duggar’s Children Samuel Scott Dillard And Israel David Dillard

Jill and Derrick have two sons, Israel(b.2015) and Samuel(b.2017).

In April of 2015, they welcomed their first child together. His parents worked for Dillard Family Ministries in Central America, where he spent his first two years of life.

In July 2017, Jill and Derick welcomed their second child, Samuel Scott, soon before leaving Central America and returning to their home state of Arkansas.
