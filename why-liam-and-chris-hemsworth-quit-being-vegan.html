Did Liam And Chris Hemsworth Quit Being Vegan Because It Hurt Their Careers?

Chris and Liam Hemsworth are two of the most famous actors in the world, and, as everyone and their dog knows, they also just so happen to be brothers.The Hemsworth brothers have gotten to such a high point in part because of how well they take care of their bodies. In addition to having such crazy and extensive workouts, they also pay very close attention to their diets. They both have experimented with vegan diets, and it appears they've had pretty good success. But how has going vegan affected their ability to get roles?At first glance, an actor going vegan shouldn't really affect their fame in any way. However, because of the importance of their body appearance, there really might be repercussions for the diets they choose. Here's everything to know.

6 What Movies Has Chris Hemworth Gone Vegan For?

Chris Hemsworth has converted to a vegan diet at multiple points throughout his career. Specifically, he's gone vegan for the movies Thor and the 2012 Avengers film, two of the many movies where he played the God of Thunder.

Chris also went vegan when filming for Avengers: Endgame. He even trained with famous vegan Torre Washington. Beyond that, he hasn't gone solely vegan when filming for specific roles. However, there was one time when he converted to vegan - for just a day.

This was the case for Thor: Love And Thunder. He was filming a kiss scene that day with Natalie Portman, who is famous for being vegan since she was 9 years old. Aside from that day, Chris stuck to his regular mixed diet - albeit a more intense version of it.

5 Is Liam Hemsworth Still A Vegan?

While Chris has gone vegan at sporadic points throughout his career, Liam became vegan for a solid period of time. This started in 2015 but then ended at some point in 2019. The reason for that is because of a kidney stone that doctors found, which Liam believes actually stemmed from his vegan diet. He did state that the vegan diet made him feel "great", but this was years before he had the kidney stone.

4 What Roles Has Chris Hemsworth Lost Out On?

Chris has primarily lost out on two roles throughout his career, and none of them seemingly have to do with him being vegan.

Chris has stated in interviews that he auditioned for undisclosed roles for the X-Men and GI: Joe franchises. This was before he won the role as Thor. Now, it seems there isn't a role Chris can't get.

Related: What Happened Between Dave Bautista And Chris Hemsworth?

As for his diet, his roles in Thor and The Avengers have proved that someone can be a vegan and still be pretty ripped. He hasn't ever explained why, despite how ripped he is, he hasn't fully converted to vegan. Perhaps he fears he'll lose out on future roles because of his diet.

3 Why Did Liam Hemsworth Quit Being Vegan?

While going vegan hasn't forced Liam to lose any roles, it has still affected his film career. In 2019, Liam actually passed a kidney stone. His kidney stone did affect his press tour for his film Isn't It Romantic. Liam claims this as the reason for finally ending his vegan diet.

A famous role that Liam did lose out on happened to be Thor - his brother's most iconic role.

2 Here's How Much Money Chris Hemworth Made From His Fitness App Centr

Both of these brothers have actually invested a lot of their money into their diet and exercise routines. Chris has even created an app called Centr, which is described by the company as a "digital fitness platform". It was bought by Jeff Bezo's brother Mark for an evaluation of over $200 million.

Chris Hemsworth actually has a total net worth of $130 million. His most expensive purchase is his giant Byron Bay mansion, and who knows how else Chris will spend his fortune in the future. Regardless, both of these brothers spend as much money as they possibly can on getting the best dietitians and exercise equipment.

1 What Roles Do Liam And Chris Have In The Future?

It seemed for a while that Chris and his career could go nowhere but up. After Thor: Love And Thunder, Chris seemed slated to appear as Thor in more Marvel movies. According to IGN, however, if he does return, it will be because the MCU writers "close the book" on Thor's story arc. As sad as that would be for fans, something even sadder has come out about Chris. He recently announced he has a risk for Alzheimer's disease, and will take a break from acting.

Liam, meanwhile, is making another shot at blockbuster acting with his appearance in the Witcher show. He's starring as the main character Geralt. That does mean he has replaced Henry Cavill, whose apparent disappearance from the show might be because of his return as Superman.

Regardless, it seems that both of these brothers are about to start a new chapter in their life journeys.
