Safaree Wants You To Know That He Did Not Write Nicki Minajs Rhymes

Sitting down with The Breakfast Club to air out some dirty laundry, Nicki Minaj’s ex-boyfriend Safaree “SB” Samuels stirred things up. While initially revealing that he assisted the Young Money rapstress in her songwriting, Safaree took to Twitter to clear the air. “Don’t say I don’t have no talent, because every time it came to writing raps and doing music, it was me, her and the beat,” he said on the radio. “She don’t do it by herself.” After a little over two weeks, Safaree is over the slander from the Barbs (who can be downright ruthless) and sought to put all the rumors to rest. Apologizing to Nicki, her former beau denounced claims that he was her ghostwriter, calling he “the most talented person” he knows. –– Cook T.P. Peep Safaree’s Twitter apology to Nicki Minaj below:
