AGT: Who Is Viviana Rossi? Talented Spanish Girl Dazed Judges Showing Her Aerial Act

 Rossi’s age has piqued people’s interest. She is 26 years old America’s Got Talent’s newest star, with her aerial act this week heating the AGT platform and viewers.

Viviana Rossi, a Spanish beauty, was a pleasant addition to this week’s audition rounds.

When she walked in, everyone was taken aback, and she advanced to the following round.

Rossi has already played on a large stage, including in Italy’s ‘Tu Si Que Vales’ in 2019, which Rossi considers one of her most unforgettable experiences.

Who Is Viviana Rossi?

At the age of 26, Viviana Rossi is a young woman displaying her gorgeous aerial stunts.

She was born in Barcelona, Spain, on February 16, 1996.

Rossi is an incredible woman who motivates other women to be as bold as she is and to overcome any injury or hurdle to achieve their goals.

Her grace as a sensual woman on stage has gotten her a lot of attention, and her aerial Talent is the icing on the cake.

Viviana Rossi Origin: Where Is She Actually From?

Viviana Rossi is from an Italian family with a history of circus acts.

According to Viaggi News, her family has been a member of the circus for centuries.

Her grandpa was a magician, and her father, Enni Rossi, works as a circus trainer. After her marriage to Enni, her mother, Gloria Jiminez, joined their line of entertainers.

As a result, the AGT star competitor had always been a part of anything unusual, especially when it came to aerial routines with specific rims and belts.

She is currently employed by Cirque du Soleil, an entertainment organization, and earns a livelihood from her work. The Italian-Spanish Rossi family is pleased with her AGT accomplishments in Spain.

Viviana Rossi Biography

Viviana Rossi identified herself as a professional aerial performer from Spain on the AGT stage.

She wowed the judging panel and the audience, who clapped for her right away. Rossi’s accomplishment is that she has traveled to numerous nations and performed there.

Similarly, the Spanish aerialist had long wanted to perform on an American stage and couldn’t think of a finer venue than AGT.

Rossi also disclosed that her AGT appearance marks her return to the stage after an onstage injury a few years ago.

Rossi’s performance included fantastic aerial stunts with water and a bathtub, which Judge Simon Cowell described as “unique.” As expected, Rossi, who is both attractive and talented, walked away from the stage with four yeses.

Her performance has been dubbed “unique aerialist” by Talent Recap.
