Khloe Kardashian Takes Care Of Dream As Blac Chyna Faces Complications Post-Surgery

Highlights

  • Dream Kardashian is spending more time at her aunt Khloe's house after her mother Blac Chyna's surgery complications.
  • Chyna opened up about complications from her breast reduction surgery, including encapsulation of one of her breasts.
  • Despite the complications, Chyna is happy with her decision and part of the "Itty Bitty Tittie Committee."

It appears Dream Kardashian is spending even more time at her aunt Khloe Kardashian’s home following her mother Blac Chyna’s surgery complications. Chyna underwent a breast reduction last year, but opened up this month about the complications occurred when the procedure didn’t go as planned.

Blac Chyna Says She Had Intense Pain After The Surgery

"Hey guys, Happy New Year's. Yep, I did it again,” she wrote in a black-and-white video uploaded to Instagram. In the clip, Chyna was wearing a compression band on her upper chest, a part of her post surgery care. She sported a makeup-free face and hid her hair underneath a beanie.

"So, earlier this year, I was like, 'You know what, I wanna take out my boobs. I'm over it I'm kinda tired of the fake look they're too big for my body, too big for my frame and it just doesn't fit me as a person anymore,’” she explained.

She proceeded to reveal that things took a turn for the worse soon after. "One of the worst things that could happen happened. One of my breasts got encapsulated," Chyna explained. "This is something that can happen whenever you do surgery."

Explaining what happened, the reality star her left breast muscle "had contracted around the implant," leading to intense pain. "All of the times I've had my breasts done, it's never happened," she added.

Despite the experience, the mom-of-two says she doesn’t regret going under the knife. "All in all, though, I'm very very happy," Chyna concluded. "I'm excited because I'm finally part of the Itty Bitty Tittie Committee.”

The short-term recovery from a breast reduction is usually 3 weeks, though in the long-term, it can take 3 to 6 mons for the body to fully heal. Chyna didn’t mention how the complications affected her recovery, including whether it kept her away from her children from longer than anticipated.

Chyna shares her youngest child, daughter Dream, with her ex-fiancé Rob Kardashian. Even though they reportedly split custody (after years of high-profile legal issues), reports have said Dream spends a lot of time with her aunt Khloe Kardashian, at least when she’s supposed to be in her father’s care.

Khloe Says Dream Is With Her Most Of The Time

Khloe seemed to imply Dream is with her much of the time during a Keeping Up with the Kardashians episode, where the family reflected on the $100 million lawsuit Chyna brought against them (though it was later dismissed).

"She's suing us for over $100 million and we're leaving our fate in the hands of 12 random people, what if they hate us?" Khloe told her older sister Kim Kardashian on camera.

“Facts are facts; right is right, wrong is wrong,” she continued. “I’m not worried about something I’ve done, I’m just saying it’s going to be stressful and annoying.”

Khloe then exclaimed, “While she’s suing us, Dream’s at my house!”

.Chyna recently settled a custody battle with Tyga, whom she shares her older child, son King Cairo, 10, with. Though he was seeking primary custody, the exes agreed to split joint and legal custody. It’s unclear if Chyna’s custody situation with Rob is being challenged at the moment.
