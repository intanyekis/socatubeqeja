Claes Bang Parents: Meet Mimi Bang, Bjarne Bang

Claes Bang was born to his parents, Mimi Bang and Bjarne Bang. His parents had divorced when he was young, and Bang ended up growing up with his father.

Not much information is available about his parents.

Who Is Claes Bang?

Claes Bang is an actor and musician best known for appearing as Christian in the film The Square (2017), and as Fjölnir in The Northman (2022).

Born on April 28, 1967, in Odense, Denmark, Bang attended the Danish National School of Theatre.

Bangs gained prominence after starring in the Danish 1997 TV show Taxa.

Since then, he has starred in various Danish TV series and films such as the Danish police drama “Anna Pihl, Küstenwache, SOKO Wismar, Notruf Hafenkante, Sibel & Max, and Cologne P.D., Jungle Child, Rettet Raffi!, Alle unter einer Tanne, and Überleben an der Scheidungsfront.”

In 2019, he appeared in the final season of The Affair, and the following year, he acted in the BBC/Netflix series Dracula (2020).

Bang is the first Danish actor to win the European Film Award for Best Actor.

As a singer, Bang is known for the singles Don’t Come Crying”, “Shameless”, and “Run”. He did a collaboration with musician Marina Schiptjenko, and they both released “Who Am I”, “I Don’t Care” and “Here We Go”.
