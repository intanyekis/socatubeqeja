Sharron Munson has a stake in her father’s business.

Sharron’s father, Lamar Hunt, was a successful businessman who made significant investments in sports and real estate. He controlled the National Football League’s Kansas City Chiefs, the Major League Soccer’s Kansas City Wizards, and FC Dallas, as well as a minority investment in the NBA’s Chicago Bulls.

In addition, he founded the Worlds of Fun and Oceans of Fun theme parks in Kansas City. In addition, he built SubTroplis, an underground business complex in the same city. As of September 2019, the total value of all of these enterprises is projected to be $3 million.

Sharron and her siblings, including her half-brothers Clark and Daniel, share equally in their father’s fortune. It is reported that they each hold 24.5 percent, with her stepmother Norma Hunt owning the remaining 2 percent. Sharron owns a portion of her father’s company but is not personally involved. Clark Hunt, her half-brother, manages the majority of the corporation. She, on the other hand, is preoccupied with her genuine love, which is art.
