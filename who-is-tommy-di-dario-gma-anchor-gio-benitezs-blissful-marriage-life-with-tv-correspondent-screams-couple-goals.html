LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: ‘’ weekend anchor Gio Benitez has a special someone in his life who has stood the test of time and has been with him for seven years. Who is Tommy DiDario?

Tommy DiDario is a TV Host & Correspondent on ‘Extra' and ‘The Rachael Ray Show.’ He covers everything from celebrity interviews to human interest stories, to lifestyle topics in fashion, travel, health, and wellness. He is also the Host and Executive Producer of his new iHeartMedia show, 'I’ve Never Said This Before,' streaming on iHeartRadio, Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and wherever else you get your podcasts. Tommy and Gio have been married for seven years and the former came onto the set of ‘GMA’ to hug Gio when he made it to the position of a weekend anchor at ‘GMA.'

Tommy is a successful podcaster who is produced by iHeartMedia and The Elvis Duran Group. Tommy’s cheerful interview with ‘Oppenheimer’ cast

Tommy has made a name for himself for his candid yet comforting interviews with actors and other celebrities. Even his podcast poses some interesting questions and guests who can tickle anyone’s fancy. Only recently, Tommy sat down with Matt Damon and Emily Blunt to talk about their movie ‘Oppenheimer’ where he broke the ice by asking both, “What makes you both such good friends?” Matt instantly bursts into laughter at Tommy’s upfront question as Emily sarcastically quizzed Matt about that question. Tommy’s question set the tone for a breezy interview with fans complimenting the interviewer for the fun interview. Tommy even managed to interview the director of the film, Christopher Nolan, showcasing his true worth as an entertainment correspondent.
