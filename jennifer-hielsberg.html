Meet Jennifer Hielsberg wife of the head coach of University of Illinois, Bret Bielema

Jennifer Hielsberg is an American model and Businesswoman. Having a well-maintained body and good facial expressions she joined the modeling world at a very young age and gathered a specific amount of fan followers. However, after exchanging her wedding vows with Bret Bielema she came into more limelight. 

Husband, Bret is an American football coach and former player who is currently assigned as the head coach for the University of Illinois. Previously, he has served for the Iowa, Kansas State, Wisconsin, Arkansas, New England Patriots, and New York Giants as head coach. While coaching at Wisconsin he set the record by winning ten games in his first season. Similarly, he was captain during his playing career at the University of Iowa.

Billionaire Robert Kraft is the owner of the New England Patriots.

Engagement with Coach, Bret Bielema

Jennifer and Bielema met one another for the very first time back in early 2000. Both of them were in the hype of their career. Reports have claimed that it was a blind date that was set up by their mutual friends during their first encounter. After knowing one another for a couple of months the pair started dating and soon involved in a serious relationship. 

On 1st April, 20011, Bret announced that he is engaged on a radio show on WTSO on Friday morning. Fans from all around the world congratulate the couple for the new move in their relationship. Later on 9th April, he threw an engagement party for his friends and family. Numerous of the world-class players and coaches were presented to the following party.

Two beautiful daughters with Bret Bielema

Within a year of their engagement, the pair accepted one another as husband and wife on 10th March 2012. The beautiful wedding ceremony was held at Madison. The bride stole the show with her beautiful white gown while the groom looks dashing in his black tuxedo. As time has now passed they are bounded in a nuptial relationship for more than eight years and are parents of two beautiful daughters. 

The first daughter, Briella Bielema was born in 2017 while the second daughter, Brexli Bielema was born in 2019. Mr. and Mrs. Bielema often uploads cute pictures of their daughters on their respective social media account. Despite having a hectic schedule, Bret still manages to spend quality time with his two princesses.

Net worth, Husband's fortune, and contract signings

Jennifer is not only a beautiful woman but she is also of hardworking nature. Her estimated net worth is around $800,000 as of 2020 which she earned through her professional modeling career and involvement in various businesses. Talking about Bret he has a net worth of over $8 Million as of 2020.

As he is signed as the head coach of Illinois for five years he is getting an annual salary of $4.2 Million. Previously he signed an eight years contract with Arkansas University where he earned over $28 Million during that time he used to earn over $3.5 Million only as a salary. 

Furthermore, he has a beautiful mansion in Fayetteville which price is ranged over $2 Million. Being one of the successful coaches he is enjoying a lavish lifestyle for sure.
