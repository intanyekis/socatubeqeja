Game Of Thrones: The Main Casts Age, Height, &amp; Relationship Status

A certain show called Game of Thrones might have ended last year, but it is one series that will stick with fans forever. The main characters were cast exceptionally well as each one helped to bring the story to life. These cast members have moved on with their lives since its end and fans are still curious about their personal lives after the show.

This list seeks to lay out the key facts about each of the main characters as fans are curious to know their age, height, and current relationship status. They have continued on in their own lives as they transition into new roles and adapt to change. Keep reading to learn these key facts about a few favorite characters from the Game of Thrones series!

10 Alfie Allen (Theon Greyjoy)

Alfie Allen played the role of Theon Greyjoy. He is 5 feet 9 inches tall. Allen is also about to turn 34 years old next month and is rumored to be in a relationship with Antonia Showering.

There is quite a bit of controversy surrounding his love life as he split from his girlfriend just seven months after the birth of their daughter.

9 Iain Glen (Jorah Mormont)

Iain Glen was cast in the role of Jorah Mormont and is one of the older cast members on this list at 59 years old. He is quite tall as he stands at 6 feet 1 inch and is currently married to Charlotte Emmerson.

The pair have two daughters together, but Glen also has a son from a previous marriage to Susannah Harker which ended in 2004.

8 Peter Dinklage (Tyrion Lannister)

Tyrion Lannister was portrayed by Peter Dinklage in this series and he is the shortest member of the cast at just 4 feet and 4 inches tall.

He is a bit older than he appears as he is now 51 years old and married to a woman named Erica Schmidt. She works as a theater director and they have two children together, with their most recent being born in 2017.

7 John Bradley (Samwell Tarly)

John Bradley played the role of Samwell Tarly who fans adored in the series. He is now 31 years old and stands at 5 feet and 8 inches tall.

6 Nikolaj Coster-Waldau (Jamie Lannister)

Jamie Lannister was portrayed by a man named Nikolaj Coster-Waldau who is currently 50 years old. He also stands at 6 feet and 2 inches tall and has been married to Nukaaka Coster-Waldau who is a singer and actress.

The pair have two daughters together and they have a strong marriage that fans can tell is meant to last a lifetime.

5 Lena Headey (Cersei Lannister)

Cersei Lannister was a special kind of woman and Lena Headey did her justice when she undertook this huge role. This actress is 46 years old and stands at 5 feet and 5 inches tall.

She recently married Dan Cadan, a writer and producer, back in 2018 and they have one daughter together. Headey also has another child from a previous marriage to Peter Paul Loughran and many are surprised she remarried after her difficult divorce.

4 Emilia Clarke (Daenerys Targaryen)

Emilia Clarke played the role of the infamous Daenerys Targaryen and her portrayal left fans obsessed with this character. Clarke is currently 33 years old and stands 5 feet and 2 inches tall, making her one of the shortest women in the series.

It is believed that she is currently single, but she has dated several actors in the past like James Franco and Seth McFarland. Fans are glad she is still alive and well after suffering two brain hemorrhages during the filming of this series.

3 Maisie Williams (Arya Stark)

Maisie Williams is the youngest on this list at just 23 years old, but it shouldn't come as a surprise seeing how she was cast as Arya Stark.

She is also the shortest woman, standing 5 feet and 1 inch tall. Williams is currently dating Reuben Selby who has founded his own modeling agency, which explains the pair's exceptional sense of fashion.

2 Sophie Turner (Sansa Stark)

Sophie Turner has been in the news quite a lot lately as she married Joe Jonas and recently gave birth to her first child.

She played the role of Sansa Stark on the show and is now 24 years old. Turner is quite tall as she stands at 5 feet and 9 inches. Fans are anxious to see which traits her newborn inherits from her as she grows older.

1 Kit Harington (Jon Snow)

Kit Harington was cast in the role of Jon Snow, who was a favorite among fans of the series. He is now 33 years old and stands at 5 feet and 8 inches tall. Harington married his love interest in the series, who is an actress named Rose Leslie.

The pair are utterly adorable together as they support one another off the set as their careers continue to trend upward.
