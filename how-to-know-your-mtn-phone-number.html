How to Know Your MTN Phone Number: A Simple Guide

Knowing your MTN phone number is essential for making calls, sharing your contact information, and staying connected with family and friends.

Whether you’ve recently acquired a new SIM card or temporarily forgot your number, MTN provides convenient methods to find out your phone number quickly.

In this article, we will walk you through the various ways to know your MTN phone number.

  • How to Know MTN Phone Number via USSD Code

    • Using the MTN USSD code is the most straightforward and quickest way to find out your phone number. Follow these steps

    • Dial *123# on your MTN mobile phone.
    • A menu will appear on your screen with several options.
    • Select “Account Information” or “My Account” from the menu.
    • Choose “My Number” or “Check Phone Number” from the next set of options.
    • Your MTN phone number will be displayed on the screen.

  • How to Know MTN Phone Number via Call

    • If you prefer to hear your phone number verbally, you can use this method:

    Dial a friend or family member’s phone number you trust and ask them to read your phone number back to you.

    Alternatively, you can use a landline or another phone to call your MTN number and check the caller ID display to see your number.

  •  via SMS

    • You can also find out your MTN phone number through SMS. Follow these steps

    • Compose a new SMS from your MTN mobile phone.
    • Type any message or keyword (e.g., “CHECK” or “NUMBER”) in the message field.
    • Send the SMS to any phone number you have access to, such as your friend’s phone.
    • When your friend receives the message, your MTN phone number will be displayed as the sender.

  • MTN Mobile App

    • If you have the MTN mobile app installed on your smartphone, you can use it to know your phone number. Follow these steps

    • Open the MTN mobile app on your smartphone or tablet.
    • Log in using your MTN account credentials.
    • Once logged in, navigate to the “My Account” or “My Profile” section.
    • Your MTN phone number will be displayed in the app.

  • How to Know MTN Phone Number via Customer Service

    • If the above methods are not feasible, you can contact MTN customer service for assistance.

    Follow these steps

    • Dial 180 from any MTN line to reach MTN customer service.
    • Speak to a customer service representative and provide them with your details for verification.
    • The customer service representative will then provide your MTN phone number.

    Knowing your MTN phone number is essential for staying connected and managing your communications effectively. With the various methods provided by MTN, you can quickly find out your phone number through USSD codes, SMS, the MTN mobile app, or by contacting customer service.

    Choose the method that is most convenient for you and always be in the know about your MTN phone number.
