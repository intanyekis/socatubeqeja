How did Joy Taylor start her Professional Career?

Taylor started her career as a public broadcaster for her university’s radio station, WBRY 1640 AM. Before joining Fox Sports in 2016, Taylor participated in Thursday Night Live and Fantasy Football Today on CBSSports.com.

Before being a co-host of “The Ticket Morning Show,” a morning sports public broadcast on 790 AM The Ticket, she most recently worked at 790 AM The Ticket where she served as a chief host. Additionally, on March 17, 2016, she appeared as a guest journalist on the radio show “The Herd with Colin Cowherd” on Fox Sports 1.

Joy has also joined the Skip and Shannon Undisputed sports debate show on Fox Sports 1 as a mediator alongside analysts Skip Bayless, a former CBS Sports examiner, and Shannon Sharpe, a former NFL tight end. In addition, Joy hosts The Hang on Facebook Live and “Perhaps I’m Crazy” on the web.

Also announced by Fox was Taylor’s transition from Undisputed to The Herd with Colin Cowherd, which would begin streaming on FS1 and Fox Sports Radio on June 18.

On September 9, 2021, Fox Sports Radio announced that Joy Taylor would serve as the anchor of her own Saturday broadcast, The Joy Taylor Show. JoyTaylorTalks is where Taylor also blogs. In this podcast, Joy discusses leisure, sports, and way of life-related topics.
