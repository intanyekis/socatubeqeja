Masked Republic and Boss Fight Studio Reveal Upcoming Lucha Libre Action Figures (Photos)

Masked Republic and Boss Fight Studio recently revealed new images of their upcoming Legends of Lucha Libre action figures!

Legends of Lucha Libre Premium Wave 2

The next wave of the Legends of Lucha Libre Premium figures includes:

  • Konnan
  • Lady Maravilla

These figures will come with:

  • 32+ points of articulation
  • Accessories
  • Interchangeable heads and hands
  • Packaged in a Window Box

These are estimated to release Q3 in 2022.

Legends of Lucha Libre Luchacitos Wave 1:

Boss Fight and Masked Republic also recently revealed their brand new Luchacitos line, which are stylized mini 3-inch figures of the Lucha Libre stars! There will also be chase versions, too!

The first wave includes:

  • Penta 0M
  • Rey Fenix
  • Konnan
  • Lady Maravilla
  • Solar
  • Tinieblas Jr

They each feature:

  • 5 points of articulation
  • Accessories

These are expected to release in Q4 of 2022!
