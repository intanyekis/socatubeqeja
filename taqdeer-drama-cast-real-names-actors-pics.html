Taqdeer Drama Cast Real Names &amp; Actors Pics

Taqdeer is a Pakistani Ary Digital drama with Alizeh Shah and Sami Khan in the lead cast. The 2022 drama Taqdeer story is full of romance, emotions, suspense, and entertainment. The drama story is written by Doorway Entertainment and directed by Mohsin Talat. Taqdeer drama is based on the story of a girl’s suffering after her wedding. Let’s look at the Taqdeer drama cast, actors and actress real name, pics, and other details.

Taqdeer Drama Cast

Alizeh Shah (Romaisa aka Roomi)

Alizeh Shah is a young and talented Pakistani drama actress and model. She started her acting career at the age of 16 years and won the heart of viewers with her charming personality. In the drama Taqdder, Alizeh Shah performed the role of Romaisa aka Roomi. Romaisa is an innocent and beautiful girl, who faces many problems in her life after the wedding. Ehd-e-Wafa is among the super hit dramas of Alizeh Shah.

Sami Khan (Asad)

Sami Khan is a well-known actor, he appeared in many dramas in lead roles. In this serial, he is performing the lead role of Asad opposite Alizeh Shah. Viewers appreciate their on-screen couple and amazing chemistry. “Mohlat” and “Tinkay Ka Sahara” are his latest dramas.

Asim Mehmood (Arsal)

Asim Mehmood is performing the role of Arsal, the elder brother of Roomi. Arsal is a very humble and caring brother who wants to do anything for his sister’s happiness. Recent dramas of Asim Mehmood are “Rung Mahal” with Seher Khan.

Aadil Hussain Dar (Haris)

Aadil Hussain is performing the role of the brother of Roomi named Haris. This is the debut drama of Aadil Hussain. He started his career as a model and moved to acting.

Amna Malik (Humna)

Amna Malik is performing the role of sister-in-law of Roomi named Humna. She appeared in many hit dramas in supporting roles. She is known for her attractive personality and incredible acting in Pakistani Urdu dramas.
