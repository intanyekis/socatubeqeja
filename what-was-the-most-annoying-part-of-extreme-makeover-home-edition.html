What was the most annoying part of Extreme Makeover Home Edition?

During each episode of Extreme Makeover Home Edition, it was exciting to see what the show could do for people. However, there was one part of the show that always was annoying, what the crew would do in the younger kids’ rooms. When a child is 5 or 6 years old, for example, they may love pirates and be incredibly excited to have a room focused on that. However, it wouldn’t take long for those same children to grow up only a little bit and care about other things much more. Then those kids would be stuck with a monstrosity of a room that would take a lot of time and effort to update.
