Spend July 4th With Fabolous At La Marina, NYC

Independence Island at La Marina featuring Fabolous, DJ Clue, and DJ Spade + Sebjak
This 4th of July, BDBG Marketing is presenting a killer beach party event on one of Manhattan’s sexiest beach clubs. La Marina is the hidden jewel of the city located directly on the Hudson River, and provides patrons with a chance to escape without leaving the comforts of home. Guests will be transported into an island-like paradise that combines music by top DJ talent with luxury beach club. VIP service will be available and feature wonderful waterfront cabana day beds. La Marina is a unique experience and ultimate destination for those seeking an exclusive and incomparable location for 4th of July.

Music will feature House and EDM during the day with Swedish DJ, Sebjak who was recently signed to the famed Ultra Records and recently collaborated with Swedish House Mafia’s Steve Angello on the track “Gods”.

Hip-Hop will come alive at night as superstar recording artist Fabolous will play host for the evening alongside music by DJ Clue and DJ Spade. Hosted by Fabolous

Location: La Marina, 348 Dyckman St, New York NY 10034
Event time: 3pm-1am
