Report: Matt Menard And Angelo Parker Sign AEW Contract Extensions

2point0 is reportedly staying with AEW.

Sean Ross Sapp of Fightful Select reports that and have signed contract extensions with the company.

The duo, formerly known as Angelo Parker and Matt Menard, debuted in AEW on August 4, 2021, and it was later announced that they signed full-time deals on August 19. Parker and Menard have been featured members of the roster ever since. They joined the Jericho Appreciation Society in March and were on the winning team in the Anarchy in the Arena match at AEW Double or Nothing 2021.

Menard suffered an injury during the Blood & Guts match on June 29, and he has been doing commentary in recent weeks. Both men have appeared on AEW TV despite this injury. Sapp reports that, according to company sources, the two men have been “good to work with” since they arrived in the company.

Prior to their arrivals in AEW, they competed as Ever-Rise in WWE NXT. They were released ten days after Fightful Select reported that the duo signed new contracts on June 15, 2021.
