Ninel Herrera Conde Wiki Biography

Ninel Herrera Conde was born on 29 September 1970, in Toluca, State of Mexico, Mexico, and is a singer, television host, and actress, best known for her performance in “Rebelde”. She’s also been a part of “Fuego en la sangre” and “Mar de amor”, but all her efforts during a career which began in 1995 have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Ninel Conde? As of late-2016, sources inform us of a net worth that is at $8 million, mostly earned through her various musical endeavors. She was also a part of the telenovela “Porque el amor manda”, and has also recorded numerous songs. She’s also collaborated with other artists, and as she continues her career it is expected that her wealth will increase.

Ninel Conde Net Worth $8 million

Ninel started becoming popular after releasing her first self-titled album in 2003, which included a duet with singer Jose Manuel Figueroa entitled “Callados”. The success of the album led to a Latin Grammy nomination in 2004 under the category “Mejor album grupero”. In the same year, she became part of TV’s “Big Brother VIP” however was voted off after 43 days. She then started an acting career, and was part of the series “Rebelde” which increased her net worth significantly; the series was produced by Pedro Damian and helped Ninel rise to prominence. She would stay with the show until 2006 and its popularity led her to become well-known in other countries, including Brazil, Romania, Colombia, Croatia, and Spain. The show also received numerous nominations, including for a Best Lead Actress nomination during the TVy Novelas 2006.

Conde also appeared in several films including “7 Mujeres”, “Mujeres Infieles 2”, and “Un Homosexual y Carlos”. She released her second album in 2005 entitled “La Rebelde” which featured several popular songs. Afterwards, she was cast in the series “Ugly Betty” in which she portrays a telenovela actress. Afterwards, she would compete in the reality show entitled “El Show de los Suenos”, which also featured Kalimba Marichal, Mariana Seoane, and Pee Wee. In 2008, she was cast in “Fuego en la sangre” alongside Eduardo Yanez and Adela Noriega. The show would also get a nomination for “Best Actress Co-star”.

In 2009, Conde appeared in the telenovela “Mar de amor” as the villain in the story, which brought another nomination, as “Best Actress Antagonistic”. A few of her latest projects include the soap opera “Porque el Amor Manda” and “Mira Quien Baila” on which she is a judge. She also released an album in 2011 entitled “Ayer y Hoy”. With her continued endeavors, Conde’s net worth continues to rise.

For her personal life, it is known that Ninel was married to actor Ari Telch from 1996 to 1998, with whom she has a daughter. In 2007, she married businessman Juan Zepada and their marriage lasted until their separation in 2013. Later in the year, she had a relationship with businessman Giovanni Medina, and they had a son in 2014.
