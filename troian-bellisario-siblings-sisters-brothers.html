Troian Bellisario Siblings, Sisters, Brothers

Troian Bellisario Siblings/Sisters/Brothers– In this article, we are going to talk about American actress Troian Bellisario’s brothers and sisters including a younger brother, three half-sisters, two half-brothers, and two stepbrothers. 

Troian Bellisario is an American actress. She received the recognization after playing the role of Spencer Hastings in the Freeform drama series Pretty Little Liars for which she won multiple awards and nominations.

She made her acting debut at the age of 3 in the 1988 film Last Rites which was directed by her father.

Troian Bellisario Siblings

Troian Bellisario has 8 siblings including a brother, two half-brothers, three half-sisters, and two stepbrothers.

Troian Bellisario Biological Brother

Troian Bellisario has a biological younger brother named Nicholas Bellisario who was born on 26 August 1991 in Los Angeles, California, USA. Troian is an actor and appeared in Monday, JAG, and Girlfriends.

Troian Bellisario Sisters

Troian Bellisario has three half-sisters named Julie B. Watson, Leslie Bellisario-Ingham, and Joy Bellisario-Jenkins.

Julie B. Watson appeared in Dimensions. Julie was born to her father Donald P. Bellisario and mother Margaret Schaffran.

Troian Bellisario Brothers
