Kris Jenner Refuses to See Kendall Jenner's Nipple Piercing

She just can’t! It’s been more than a year since Kendall Jenner’s nipple piercing made its debut, but her mom, Kris Jenner, still isn’t over it.

“I must tell you the truth: The only place I’ve ever seen it is in a picture somewhere on the Internet or in a magazine where it showed through,” Jenner, 60, told Kyle Sandilands and Jackie O, of KIIS FM’s The Kyle & Jackie O Show, on Friday, July 15. “So I’ve never seen it in person.”

The momager continued that while she could be a bigger fan of her daughter’s choice of piercing, she picks her battles wisely. “I have to gripe at them about so much that they would never want to talk to me if I just was that person,” she said.

That said, Kris still can’t believe that Kendall, 20, arguably the most conservative member of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, actually took the plunge on the NSFW piercing. “It’s not [like] Kendall! That’s why it was so surprising,” she said. “I thought, ‘This is not real.’ And then I just let it go. I thought, ‘I don’t even want to know’ I’m in denial.”
