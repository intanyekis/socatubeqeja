ThriftCon Is Coming To Portland For The First Time Ever

ThriftCon, the world’s premier vintage clothing and collectible convention, is coming to Portland for the first time ever on Sunday, August 27, 2023. The event will take place at the Portland Expo Center from 10 am to 5 pm.

ThriftCon is a one-stop shop for all things vintage and retro. There will be over 100 vendors selling everything from clothing and accessories to home goods and collectibles. You’ll find everything from classic Levi’s jeans to rare Elvis Presley memorabilia.

In addition to shopping, ThriftCon will also feature a variety of activities and events. There will be live music, food trucks, and a fashion show. You can also learn about the history of thrifting and sustainability in fashion at ThriftCon University. ThriftCon is a great way to find unique and affordable clothing and accessories. It’s also a fun and educational event for the whole family. Tickets are on sale now at www.thriftcon.co.

Some of the things you can expect at ThriftCon Portland

  • Over 100 vendors selling vintage clothing, accessories, home goods, and collectibles
  • Live music, food trucks, and a fashion show
  • ThriftCon University, a learning experience about the history of thrifting and sustainability in fashion
  • A kid’s zone with games, crafts, and activities

Some tips for getting the most out of ThriftCon Portland

  • Arrive early to avoid the crowds
  • Bring cash or a credit card, as many vendors do not accept checks
  • Be prepared to haggle
  • Don’t be afraid to ask the vendors questions about their items
  • Have fun and enjoy the experience!

ThriftCon Portland is a great way to find unique and affordable clothing and accessories. It’s also a fun and educational event for the whole family. Tickets are on sale now, so don’t miss out!
